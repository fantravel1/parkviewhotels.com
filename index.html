How It Works

The Calm Scoring System

Every hotel is evaluated on what matters most: your proximity to nature and your ability to decompress. No exaggeration. No misleading claims. Just verified calm.

Green Proximity

How close is the hotel to meaningful green space? Direct adjacency, walk-out access, or visual connection — measured and verified.

View Quality

Direct park view, partial green, or walk-out only? We verify the actual view from recommended rooms and floors, not marketing images.

Noise Reduction

Sound profile matters. We assess ambient noise, traffic separation, and whether you hear birds or buses from your room.

Light Quality

Natural light access, window orientation, and morning sun exposure. Does the room invite daylight and nature in?

Walkability

Can you walk into the park within two minutes? Is the path pleasant? Are crossings calm? We verify the route, not just the distance.

Overall Calm Score

A composite measure of everything above. The definitive answer to: will this hotel help me decompress? Verified, honest, no hype.

Frequently Asked Questions

Questions & Answers

What is ParkviewHotels?

ParkviewHotels is a nature-first urban hotel platform that curates hotels located directly beside parks, gardens, waterfronts, and meaningful green spaces in cities worldwide. We treat green proximity as a core amenity, not a bonus. Our mission is to help travelers find calm in cities by staying next to nature.

What makes a hotel a "Parkview Hotel"?

A Parkview Hotel must be within direct visual or walk-out proximity to publicly accessible green space — such as a city park, botanical garden, waterfront promenade, or urban forest. The green space must be suitable for walking, sitting, breathing, and reflection. We reject hotels that have token greenery or misleading "park view" claims.

How does the Calm Scoring System work?

Every hotel receives scores across five dimensions: Green Proximity, View Quality, Noise Reduction, Light Quality, and Walkability. These combine into an Overall Calm Score. Each metric is verified through our evidence system — not self-reported by hotels. Dealbreakers like misleading claims, heavy road separation, or excessive noise will disqualify a listing.

Which cities does ParkviewHotels cover?

We're launching with iconic park-city pairings: Central Park (NYC), Hyde Park (London), Stanley Park (Vancouver), Tiergarten (Berlin), Ueno Park (Tokyo), and Chapultepec (Mexico City). We plan to expand to 10,000+ cities globally, covering secondary cities, river and waterfront destinations, historic garden districts, and national-park-edge cities.

Does ParkviewHotels book rooms?

ParkviewHotels is an intelligence and discovery platform, not a booking engine. We help you find the right hotel next to the right green space. We provide detailed information about view quality, noise profiles, and walkability so you can make informed booking decisions through your preferred booking platform.

How do you verify hotel claims?

We use a multi-tier evidence system. Tier A uses verified maps and on-site validation. Tier B relies on reputable imagery and consistent traveler feedback. Tier C uses verified field reports. Hotels that cannot be adequately verified (Tier D) are never published. Every listing includes a last-reviewed date and confidence label.
